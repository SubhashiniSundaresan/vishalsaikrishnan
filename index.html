Accomplishments

What I have done

I have been able to identify my areas of passion and pursued them in a very profound and rigorous way.

Winner of International Crescendo competition

I have been playing the piano for 12 years, and have reached the Advanced Level (Level 10). I won the Crescendo International Music Competition twice, and was invited to play at Carnegie Hall, New York in February 2018 and Feb 2019. The experience was incredible and I look forward to playing at Carnegie Hall again. Crescendo Competition Winner

COSMOS participant (California State Summer school for Math and Science)

Selected to the highly selective and competitive COSMOS program in summer of 2018. An intensive summer residential program for talented students who are selected based on academic excellence to work with renowned faculty, researchers and scientists in state-of-the-art facilities. As part of the cohort for Chemistry cluster, I worked on a project to experiment on alternative sources of energy. I experimented using different ions and finally nailed the storage of electricity inside a calcium ion battery. I presented this project as a culmination of COSMOS at UC Davis in July 2018.

Founder and President of the Biology Olympiad club at Vista Del Lago High School

I founded the Biology Olympiad club at Vista, based on my interest in biology. I was intrigued to learn more about the human genes. I learnt about the world-renowned genome center at UC Davis in our vicinity. I approached Professor Justin Siegel and interviewed with him and got into his team to research protein synthesis in gene mutation.

Academic excellence

I was awarded AP scholar with distinction twice in 2018 and in 2019 in addition to National AP scholar in 2019.

CTE Preceptorship program

I was selected for the CTE (Career and Technical Education) summer preceptorship program at Vista Del Lago High School. I was placed at UC Davis Hospital for 4 weeks to learn the nuances of medical science in a real hospital setting. I participated in early morning rounds beginning at 6am in the hospital and working a full day. While there, I learnt about hospital administration and the Information technology (IT) supporting the hospital system.

My Support for Good Causes

I'm a teen proponent and youth ambassador for the Indian Association for the Blind, a non profit organization supporting the visually challenged in a remote city in Southern India. I visited this non profit organization few years back one summer and was intrigued by the visually challenged.
